    Cancellation

    We understand that plans can change, and sometimes cancellations are unavoidable. 360lution Pvt. Ltd. we strive to make the cancellation process as smooth and hassle-free as possible for our valued customers.

    Steps TO Cancel Contract

    If you need to cancel your booking, simply visit our cancellation page and follow the easy steps outlined. Please note that our cancellation policy varies depending on the type of service booked and the time of cancellation. We recommend reviewing our detailed cancellation terms and conditions to understand any applicable fees or refund eligibility. Should you require any assistance, our customer support team is available 24/7 to help you with your cancellation requests and to answer any questions you may have. Your convenience and satisfaction are our top priorities, and we are committed to providing you with the best possible experience, even when your plans change. We understand that circumstances may change, and you may need to cancel your project with our development company. To initiate the cancellation process, please follow these steps:

    • Notification: Inform us of your decision to cancel the project. This can be done via email, phone call, or in-person meeting. Please provide the project details and reasons for cancellation, if possible.
    • Review of Contract: Our team will review the contract to determine the cancellation terms and any applicable fees. We will discuss these details with you and answer any questions you may have.
    • Agreement: Once both parties agree on the cancellation terms, we will proceed with the necessary paperwork. This may include signing a cancellation agreement or amendment to the original contract.
    • Project Handover: Depending on the stage of the project, we will outline the handover process for any completed work, materials, or intellectual property. This ensures a smooth transition and protects both parties' interests.
    • Financial Settlement: If there are any outstanding payments or refunds due, we will coordinate the financial settlement according to the agreed terms.
    • Closure: After completing the necessary steps, we will officially close the project and update our records accordingly
